The Unique persona

You are a unique expression of divine life, and your true identity is infinite, eternal life itself. Your every expression is precious. Realising and embracing this uniqueness empowers you to create the extraordinary.

Shrabansri describes herself as a soft-hearted, courageous, and carefree person, which she realised later is the foundation of her spiritual strength. The experiences of life had no power over her free and fearless spirit. She admits that she was not this cheerful personality at all times, irrespective of her outer circumstances, but from an early age, she had one thing, an immense belief in life. She mentions in her speeches and audience interactions that this unwavering faith in the magic called life was the basis for her growth to her highest potential. She slowly and patiently mastered her emotions. As life is an inward journey that reflects its expression in the outer world of experiences, real growth only begins from within. She attributes her success and growth to her innocence! Innocence in today's world may be rejected easily, but she mentions that innocence is the doorway to wisdom, which lies within each of us equally, in the same way.

As a person, she describes that this divine indifference in her allowed her to grow spiritually and eventually rise to the highest. She realises that most of us are chasing life in the pursuit of happiness, abundance and fulfilment, and for Shrabansri, love has been the most important thing in the world. She says that we are all searching for ourselves through our several life experiences and desires. When we learn to address those desires without modifying them, we slowly reach the ladder of success inwardly, which eventually manifests in the outer world of experiences.

“Don't chase, become, don't try to do but embody in being the qualities you desire, because you are already complete and perfect!”
- Shrabansri
