The Unique Journey

As we are unique, our life’s journey is absolutely unique too! We are all on this beautiful journey to find ourselves and our true potential.

Shrabansri’s journey is nothing short of magnificent, leading her to serve millions of people as a global inspirational speaker and author. She has been associated with multinational corporations as an HR Professional after she completed her Master's degree in Human Resource Management from Pune University in the year 2015, but nothing seemed so fulfilling to her until life situations compelled her to look within herself when her search began for the true purpose of life, and in this inner journey, her first search was for love. She mentions that it is love that pulled her from the lowest state, where she was drowning in the ocean of life, to the highest state as infinite life itself! During this inner journey, she showed marvellous courage, persistence and determination to reach her goal. She yearned for the divine love that she had always been searching for in the outside world. With love, she realised her highest potential, and since then, there was no looking back!

“Love is the backbone of courage. What love can make you do, ego-driven motivation can never do it!”
- Shrabansri
